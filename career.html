    • Reptilian Rush:

      Reptilian Rush is a classic Snake Game implemented in C++. It provides an enjoyable and addictive gaming experience.

    • Randomly Placed Fruits:

      Throughout the game, fruits are randomly placed on the game board. By maneuvering the snake to eat these fruits, players can increase their score and the length of the snake. This adds an element of strategy and challenge to the game.

    • Pause and Resume:

      Players have the option to pause the game by pressing the 'P' key. Pressing 'P' again allows them to resume the game from where they left off. This feature is convenient for those who may need to take a break or attend to something urgent.

    • During gameplay, players can control the snake's movement using the arrow keys or WASD, eat fruits to increase their score and length, avoid collisions with walls and the snake's body, pause and resume the game by pressing 'P', and exit the game by pressing 'X'.
